## Overview

LevelApp is a specialized platform that combines marketplace functionality with social networking for the gaming community. Unlike generic marketplaces, it allows gamers to showcase their identities, preferences, and collections while facilitating buying, selling, and discussions. Users can chat with fellow gamers, search for products with advanced filters, save items, and receive notificationsâ€”all in a gamer-focused environment.

## Client Challenges

Gamers struggle with common marketplaces like eBay, Craigslist, or Mercado Libre, which lack community aspects and are not optimized for gaming needs. These platforms focus solely on transactions, making it hard to build connections, showcase personal gaming identities, or discuss games. This leads to isolated buying/selling experiences and difficulty finding niche gaming items without a dedicated space.

## Our Solution

- **Integrated Marketplace & Social Hub**: A mobile app where users can list and discover gaming products (consoles, games, accessories) with geolocation-based search, while also chatting, following profiles, and discussing games in real-time.
- **Community-Driven Features**: Profiles for showcasing gamer identities, chat for direct interactions, and tools like filters and notifications to enhance discovery and engagement.
- **Scalable Monetization**: Premium options for highlighted ads and social perks, ensuring sustainability as the user base grows.

## Key Features

- **Chat with Gamers**: Real-time messaging to connect, discuss games, and build friendships.
- **Product Discovery & Search**: Advanced filters, international geolocation, and saving products for later.
- **Social Profiles**: Showcase gaming preferences, collections, and identities (e.g., favorite games, achievements).
- **Notifications & Invites**: Push/email alerts for new listings or messages, plus easy friend invites.
- **Extensive Database**: Images, videos, and info on over 300,000 games and consoles as a social core for discussions and questions.

## Technologies & Integration

- **Mobile App**: Android (Google Play Store)
- **User Authentication**: Facebook Login Integration
- **Notifications**: Push & Email
- **Geolocation**: International Product Search & Filtering

## Results & Impact

- **User Growth**: Achieved 1,079 registered users in Germany shortly after launch, with 390 subscriptions from a 2-month beta POC.
- **Conversion & Performance**: Subscription conversion rates of 18%-27.5% (9x the tech industry average), landing page achieved "unicorn status".
- **Engagement**: High user love for the concept and positive testimonials; positioned as a unique mix of social network and niche marketplace for gamers.
